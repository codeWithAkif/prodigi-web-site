Innovative Products

AI Data Assistant

PSight

PSight is an on‑premise AI data assistant that lets organizations query their SSAS Tabular models using natural language. Without needing SQL or DAX expertise, it instantly transforms business questions into insights, delivering results as tables or visualizations while keeping all data securely within the company’s infrastructure.

Capabilities

  • Natural Language Querying: Ask questions in English or Turkish, automatically converted into DAX queries.
  • Smart Error Correction: AI reads SSAS error messages and self‑corrects faulty queries.
  • Conversation Memory: Remembers the last five turns, enabling refined follow‑up questions.
  • Automatic Visualization: Generates bar charts, line charts, or tables based on data structure.
  • Multi‑Model & Multi‑LLM Support: Connects to multiple SSAS Tabular models and works with Ollama, LM Studio, or vLLM backends.
AI Call Analytics

AUDIVISE

Audivise is an AI-powered platform that transforms call center voice recordings into actionable insights. It converts audio to text, analyzes sentiment and topics, and presents results through intuitive dashboards—helping organizations improve customer experience, boost agent performance, and drive data‑driven decisions.

Detailed Features

  • Speech-to-Text Conversion: Advanced speech recognition with multi-language support and speaker separation.
  • Text Analysis & Insights: Sentiment detection, topic identification, and agent performance scoring (ScoreCard)
  • Reporting & Visualization: Interactive dashboards, time-based analytics, and filtering by category or satisfaction.
  • End-to-End AI Integration: Conversational AI for exploring data and custom GenAI-powered insights.
  • Plug & Play Setup: Integration and data transfer can begin within just one day.
